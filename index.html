When, where, why?

I have been running for the Owen J. Roberts cross country and track teams all of my years in high school.

As a freshman I placed 40th at districts and qualified for the state championship, which I placed 84th in. In indoor track I ran at the New Balance Games in the DMR and ran a 3:20 1200m leg. Our team placed 4th. I set a mile PR of 4:41.50.

As a softmore I set a new PR of 16:02 and was consitently under the 16:40 mark. I placed 18th at districts and once again qualified for the state championship. This time I placed 38th in 16:42 on a difficult and very wet Aloha Hills course. In indoor track I ran a 4:29 as the 1600m leg of a DMR in the varsity classic meet at the Armory. I also set a mile PR of 4:33. Outdoor season was unfortunately cancelled but not before I was able to run a 53 second 400 in trainers during a time trial.

I am currently a junior and I missed all the races of my Cross Country season due to COVID-19 and then an injury. As I am writing this, our indoor season looks like it will be cancelled due to COVID-19. Hopefully I can get some good times in the spring!
